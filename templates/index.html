About Us

Trusted Traditional Wellness,
Right Here in Kano

M UMAR Natural Remedies was founded to preserve and share the traditional herbal wellness knowledge cherished across Northern Nigeria. Every remedy we prepare combines time-honored methods with carefully selected natural ingredients — giving our community safe, effective and affordable wellness solutions for everyday life.

  • Authentic herbal knowledge passed down through trusted practice.
  • Naturally sourced herbs, oils and remedies, prepared with care.
  • Trusted across Kano, serving families and individuals for years.
